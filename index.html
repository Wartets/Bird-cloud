About Bird-cloud

This simulation is based on the Boids algorithm, mimicking the flocking behavior of birds.

The 3 Rules:

  • Separation: Birds steer to avoid crowding local flockmates.
  • Alignment: Birds steer towards the average heading of local flockmates.
  • Cohesion: Birds steer to move towards the average position of local flockmates.

Interaction:

  • Predator: The mouse cursor acts as a predator. Birds will actively flee from it.
  • Tracking: Click on a bird to visualize its individual path.

Controls:

Adjust the sliders in the settings (top right) to change the flock's physics.
